Conjunction

A conjunction is a linking word used to connect two or more than two words or sentences.

Paul has a dog and a cat.

John is handsome but timid.

Is this a chicken or a goat?

Peter could speak before he was three.

I always brush my teeth after I have had my dinner.

Marie plays football since she was childhood.

Take some money with you when you go shopping.

Don’t leave until you have finished your work.

James listened to music while he was exercising.

Mrs Flower was smiling as she walked into the class.
